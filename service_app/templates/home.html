示例：

----------Instagram----------

Fetch Instagram Author Profile

http://{% print(my_host) %}/service_app?website_name=instagram&page_type=profile&target_account=bill

target输入必须是account


Fetch Instagram Author Message

http://{% print(my_host) %}/service_app?website_name=instagram&page_type=message&target_id=34&page_count=1

target输入必须是id, 目前还没支持翻页


----------Facebook----------

Fetch Facebook Author Profile

http://{% print(my_host) %}/service_app?website_name=facebook&page_type=profile&target_account=bnbarry34

target输入可以是id也可以是account


Fetch Facebook Author Message

http://{% print(my_host) %}/service_app?website_name=facebook&page_type=message&target_account=bnbarry34&page_count=1

target输入可以是id也可以是account


Fetch Facebook Author Friends

http://{% print(my_host) %}/service_app?website_name=facebook&page_type=friend&target_account=bnbarry34&page_count=1

target输入可以是id也可以是account
